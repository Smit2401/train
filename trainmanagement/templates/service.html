Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our railway services.

How do I book a train ticket?

Booking a train ticket with us is easy! You can book online through our website or mobile app, or visit any of our ticket counters at the station.

What amenities are available onboard?

We provide various amenities onboard, including comfortable seating, dining options, Wi-Fi, and entertainment. Specific amenities may vary by train type and route.

Can I bring pets on the train?

Small pets are generally allowed onboard, subject to specific guidelines and conditions. Please check our pet policy or contact us for more details.

How can I track the status of my train?

You can track your train's status in real-time through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service. We provide live updates on departure, arrival times, and delays.

What should I do if I miss my train?

If you miss your train, you may be able to reschedule your ticket depending on availability and our rescheduling policy. Contact us or visit a station for assistance.
